COUNSELLING

Owing to fast paced & highly demanding systems in terms of academics & competency, children, especially teenagers, quickly fall into the trap of depression. Lack of self awareness, situational awareness & coping mechanisms, may lead to constant apprehension, low self esteem or outburst of aggression in children. With therapeutic intervention, we cater to the child's psychological needs & offer educational counselling, family counselling and parental counselling based on individual & family needs.

Key Objectives

  • To facilitate positive behavioural change
  • To improve the child's interpersonal and intrapersonal relationship
  • To resolve internal conflicts
  • To enhance life skills

Key Benefits

  • CPersonality & behavioural analysis
  • Age appropriate intervention
  • Amicable approach towards children
  • Guidance on parenting by experts in the field.
